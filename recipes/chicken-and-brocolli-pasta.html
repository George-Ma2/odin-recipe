Chicken and Brocolli Pasta

Ingredients

  • 350g broccoli, cut into small florets, stems cut into matchsticks
  • 300g dried curly fettuccine pasta
  • 1⁄2 barbecued chicken, skin and bones removed, meat coarsely shredded
  • 2 x 80g Boursin Garlic & Herbs cheese
  • 125g (3⁄4 cup) sun-dried tomatoes, coarsely chopped

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil. Add the broccoli and cook for 2 minutes or until bright green and tender crisp. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the broccoli to a bowl. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook following packet directions or until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken, broccoli, cheese and tomato. Use a ladle to transfer 250ml (1 cup) pasta cooking water to the chicken mixture. Stir until the cheese melts. Continue to cook, tossing, for 2-3 minutes or until heated through.
  3. Drain the pasta and add to the broccoli mixture. Toss until well combined. Season.
